Welcome to Feathered Wonders!

Get ready to meet Australia's most fascinating feathered friends! From the shy but mighty Cassowary to the laughing Kookaburra and the striking Yellow-Tailed Black Cockatoo, our exhibition showcases the unique birds that call Australia home.

Cassowary: A shy rainforest giant with powerful legs.

Kookaburra: Famous for its laughing call.

Black Cockatoo: Known for its striking yellow tail.
